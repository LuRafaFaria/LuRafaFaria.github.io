// Who I am

Developer.
Tester. Collaborator.

I am a Game Developer and QA Tester with a strong foundation in Unity, C#, and cross-platform development. My background spans gameplay programming, shader authoring, 3D animation, and mobile publishing — built through two years of research at Universidade do Minho and several years of academic and personal projects.

I approach software with a "hacker mentality" — I push systems to their limits, look for where they break, and document findings clearly enough that the next person can build on them. That instinct makes me useful both as a developer and as a tester.

I recently completed a six-month Cybersecurity course covering OWASP Top 10, penetration testing concepts, IDS/IPS tooling, and Linux — which sharpens how I think about edge cases and system security.
